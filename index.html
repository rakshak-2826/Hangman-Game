How to Play

Welcome to the Hangman Game! Here's how you can play:

  • Click "Play" to start the game.
  • Select a difficulty level.
  • Pick a category from the list provided.
  • A word from the chosen category will be selected randomly.
  • You will see the word as a series of underscores (_), representing each letter of the word.
  • Use the on-screen keyboard to guess letters and numbers. Click on a character to make a guess.
  • If the guessed character is in the word, it will replace the corresponding underscores.
  • If the guessed character is not in the word, your health decreases by one.
  • You have 8 guesses (health) in total. The game ends when you either guess the entire word or run out of guesses.
  • You can pause the game at any time and choose to continue, pick a new category, or quit the game.
  • You can use up to 3 hints during the game. Each hint reveals one letter in the word.
  • Use the "New Category" button after winning or losing to choose a new category and play again.
